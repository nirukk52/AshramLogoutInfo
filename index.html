Delete or Logout from Your Account

Logout Instructions

To logout of your account:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on the Logout button
  4. Confirm when prompted

Delete Account Instructions

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the app
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm that you want to delete your account
  5. Enter your registered email and last name when prompted
  6. Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted

If you need any help with this process, please contact us at niranjan.kurambhatti@gmail.com.

Important Note

Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. All personal data associated with the account will be removed permanently.
